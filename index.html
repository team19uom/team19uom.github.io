Community Project

Summary

As human beings everyone has the social responsibilities from their born to death and it is positioning under ethical framework. In other words social responsibility is a duty of every individual or an organisation to serve for the society and filling the gaps between societies comparatively.

As the students of the Department of Mechanical Engineering at University of Moratuwa, have received a great opportunity to serve the needy people / societies especially in rural areas where the low facilities are available.

This report addresses social responsibility project carried out by a group of students to help on fulfilling the gaps between societies instead of learning theoretical concepts and practical sessions at university as frequent activities.

Introduction

Background

As university undergraduates and school levers under free education framework in Sri Lanka, every university student or school lever has the responsibility of doing something for the development of it. Based on above manner, group members have decided to convey our helping hand to increase / fabricate infrastructure facilities of a selected school in rural village. For fulfilling this purpose group members have planned to renovate and modify the water supply system, storage system, distribution network and purifying / cleaning process of water in the selected school.

Aim

Increasing the infrastructure facilities of a primary school in a rural village at Waga, Meepe, Western province by constructing the water supply system, water storage system, water distribution network and purifying / cleaning process

Objectives



  1. To check the possibility of using current water supply pressure through Rural Water and Sanitation Project, instead of adding a water pump
  2. To identifying a suitable location to locate the overhead water storage tank
  3. To plan the water distribution pipe network
  4. To propose a method of purifying / cleaning the water to be drink
